Rules

The first player to have a score of 100 or over in the permanent bank wins the game. A player will roll the dice and have the option to either end the session, whereby the score in the temporary bank gets added to the permanent bank and the next player continues. Or they can add the score to the temporary bank. They can accumulate as many dice scores as they want by continuing to roll the dice. Unfortunately if the player rolls a 1, all the accumulated scores in the temporary bank get wiped out and the session ends.
